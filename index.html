How Watcher works

Pick a 5-letter word in your head — or type it in. The AI takes six guesses to crack it. After each guess, you set the tile colours yourself:

A in your word, in the right place
B in your word, wrong place
C not in your word

The page is the AI's notebook. The panel on the right shows the model's full reasoning — what letters it has ruled in, ruled out, and why it picked the next word. No fake typing animations. Honest latency.

Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash via the Google AI Studio API. The model never sees your secret word — only its own prior guesses and the colours you confirmed.
